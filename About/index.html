OUR TEAM

This page was what started as a small idea with no immediate afterthought , “what if there was a doctor for plants?”. And this seed conspired into this multi-faceted, web application beyond our wildest expectations.
Since the birth of this project the needs of plant lovers has been kept at the core of our belief system, to make it user friendly and accessible to both novice gardeners and farmers and to make it less daunting to pick up this wonderful hobby.
ProLeafic, was meant to be not more than a simple yes or no answer form. However from conception to creation to completion through long days and sleepless nights we are and will always be very proud of what it has become and to call Proleafic our own
